Our Curriculum

1. Requirements

  • You must be at least 22 years of age.
  • You must have a minimum 1 year of professional working experience.*
  • You must be proficient in Japanese or English.
  • You must possess a bachelor’s degree, or equivalent degree.*

*If you do not have a bachelor’s degree or equivalent by the time of entrance, you will need to undergo a Preliminary Screening to determine if your background and work experience is sufficient.

3.Application Process

If you are 18 years of age but do not meet one or more of the admission requirements listed above, you may still apply for the JIPA programs. However, you will first need to pass a Preliminary Screening, which includes a review of your language skills and essays.
